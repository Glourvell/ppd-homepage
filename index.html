Screen Printing

It is a durable printing technique that pushes ink through a mesh screen to create vibrant, long-lasting designs on T-shirts, hoodies, uniforms, bags, and other fabric products.

The benefits are:

  • Vibrant Colours
  • Long Lasting
  • Cost Effective
  • Premium Quality
  • Fade Resistant

Sublimation Printing

Sublation printing delivers vibrant, long-lasting, and high-quality designs that become part of the fabric or surface itself, giving you fade-resistant prints with a smooth professional finish perfect for custom apparel, mugs, branding, and promotional products.

The benefits are:

  • Best Quality
  • Fade Resistance
  • Premium Quality
