ABOUT

US

Founded in Nakuru in 2007, Printspoint Designers has grown from a small specialized design initiative into one of the region’s most trusted names in creative branding, graphic design, and precision printing—serving clients across Kenya and beyond. Unlike one-size-fits-all print shops, we champion a tailored approach—seamlessly blending offset and digital production to meet international standards while customizing every project to reflect our clients’ unique vision and context. What sets us apart isn’t just what we print, but why we print it. Every logo, packaging concept, or branded T-shirt is created with purpose—to help our clients stand out, build connection, and perform in an increasingly visual world. Over the years, we've refined our craft to strike the perfect balance between creativity and commercial impact, combining local insight with global quality.
